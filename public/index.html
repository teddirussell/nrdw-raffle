Terms & Conditions

1. The Organiser
This raffle is conducted by Northern Rivers Dirty Wheels Mountain Bike Club Inc. (NRDW), a not-for-profit community incorporated association registered in New South Wales, Australia.

2. Eligibility
Open to residents of NSW and QLD aged 18 years and over. Committee members directly involved in organising the raffle and their immediate family members are not eligible to enter.

3. The Prize
One (1) 2018 Scott Spark 700 Tuned mountain bike (Medium frame). The prize is valued under $30,000. The prize is not transferable or exchangeable for cash. The bike is offered in its current condition — no warranty is provided.

4. Ticket Price & Purchase
Tickets are $20.00 AUD each. Tickets may be purchased online or in person at NRDW club events. All ticket sales are final — no refunds.

5. NRDW Member Bonus
Current financial NRDW members receive one (1) bonus ticket free of charge for every $50.00 spent on raffle tickets (e.g., purchase 3 tickets for $60 and receive 1 bonus ticket). Members must tick the member checkbox at the time of purchase. Membership will be verified against current AusCycling/NRDW records. Bonus tickets cannot be claimed retrospectively.

6. The Draw
The draw will take place at 2:00 PM AEST on Saturday 17 May 2026 at The Federal Hotel ("The Feddy"). The draw will be conducted publicly using a fair and random method. The winner does not need to be present to win.

7. Winner Notification
The winner will be notified by phone and/or email within two (2) business days of the draw. The winner's name will be published on the NRDW website and Facebook page. If the winner cannot be contacted or does not claim the prize within three (3) months of the draw date, a redraw will be conducted.

8. Use of Proceeds
In accordance with the NSW Community Gaming Act 2018, a minimum of 40% of gross proceeds will go directly to NRDW to support trail building, maintenance, and community mountain biking events.

9. Records
NRDW will retain records of all ticket sales, the draw process, and the winner's details for a minimum of three (3) years in accordance with NSW Fair Trading requirements.

10. Privacy
Personal information collected during ticket purchases will be used solely for the purposes of conducting this raffle and will not be shared with third parties. Contact information may be used by NRDW for winner notification only.

11. Governing Law
This raffle is conducted under and governed by the NSW Community Gaming Act 2018 and the Community Gaming Regulation 2020. No permit is required as the total prize value is under $30,000.
